Jobs of the Future:
Machine Learning!

Interested in job security? Don’t look for careers that have been popular in the past- look at careers that are up-and-coming, and explore the exciting world of being a Machine Learning Engineer!

A recent report says that “30% of surveyed U.S. managers said their company is currently using artificial intelligence and machine learning, while 53% expect to adopt these tools within the next three to five years”, and according to the job aggregate site Indeed, a job in machine learning is the best and most up-and-coming job in the United States.

So, dust off your algebra and statistics, brush up on your Python, and see which Job of the Future is right for you!
